Financial Year Dates

Choose when your financial year starts and ends. Choose a ‘Lockdown’ date to stop new transactions being saved before this.

Business Data Retention Period

Choose how long you need to keep your accounting records. We’ll use this to calculate how long you should keep your contact’s personal data. This is important for complying with data protection laws.

VAT Details

Enter details of your VAT registration, including how often you’ll submit VAT returns to HMRC. Enter your HMRC User ID to submit VAT returns directly from Accounting.

TC Accounting Solutions is a finance application for self-employed painters and decorators. TC Accounting allows industry professionals to input their financial data allowing them to fill out the required GOV.UK self-assessment tax forms. *This is a prototype used for testing purposes*
